## Spacing & Sizing

### Standard Padding
- Container: 30px
- Card Body: 20px
- Section: 12-16px

### Standard Margins
- Between sections: 20-30px
- Within section: 8-12px

### Border Radius
- Containers: 16px
- Cards: 12px
- Buttons: 8px
- Badges: 20px (pill-shaped)
