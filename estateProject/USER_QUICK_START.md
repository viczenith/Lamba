## ğŸš¨ TROUBLESHOOTING

### "A user with this email already exists!"
**Solution:** This email is already registered. Try:
1. Click **"Forgot password?"** to reset your password
2. Use a different email address
3. Contact support if you need help

### "Passwords do not match!"
**Solution:** Ensure both password fields are identical:
1. Check for typos
2. Copy-paste from password field to confirm field
3. Use the eye icon to verify passwords

### "Password must be at least 8 characters long!"
**Solution:** Create a stronger password:
1. Minimum 8 characters
2. Mix uppercase and lowercase
3. Include numbers or symbols

### "Registration failed: A record with this information already exists!"
**For Companies:**
1. Company name must be unique
2. Registration number must be unique
3. Email must be unique

**For Clients/Marketers:**
1. Email must be unique
2. Try a different email address

### Not Receiving Welcome Email?
**Check:**
1. Spam/Junk folder
2. Promotions tab (if using Gmail)
3. Email address spelling
4. Wait 5-10 minutes (email delivery delay)
